## Purpose
Welcome to the **New Controllers** section of the eAIP. This section is designed to help you get started with becoming a certified controller in the VATSSA region.

From the first steps, such as registering yourself onto the waiting list, getting the relevant software such as TrackAudio and EuroScope fully set up and operational to becoming a fully certified controller.

**We are not able to give estimated waiting times for your training, hence refrain from asking any mentors or examiners for your position.**
